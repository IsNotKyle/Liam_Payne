Biography

Liam James Payne was born 29 August 1993 at New Cross Hospital in the Heath Town district of Wolverhampton, West Midlands, England. [1] He was born three weeks early, and suffered frequent illnesses. He was best known as a member of the pop boy band "One Direction", one of the best-selling boy bands of all time. He appeared as a solo auditionee on the British television series "The X Factor" in 2008 and 2010 when he was invited to join the newly formed boy band, "One Direction", who placed 3rd and went on to achieve global success. He was never married and had one son born in 2017. While in a Buenos Aires hotel room, Liam fell from a balcony on 16 October 2024 and lost his life.
